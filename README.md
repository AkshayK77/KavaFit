# KavaFit

> Train smarter. Progress faster.

KavaFit is an AI-powered fitness coaching app that builds personalized workout plans, tracks your sessions in real time, and adapts to how you train — all in one place.

---

## Features

**Smart Workout Planning**
Generate weekly training splits tailored to your goals, available equipment, and experience level. The AI factors in your training history and adjusts over time.

**Live Session Tracking**
Log sets, reps, and weight as you train. Built-in rest timer, warm-up suggestions, and automatic personal record detection keep every session focused.

**Progress Analytics**
Visualize your performance with volume trend graphs, muscle group heatmaps, and a full history of PRs and completed sessions.

**Nutrition Tracking**
Search millions of foods, log meals, and hit your macro targets. Includes an AI recipe generator, grocery list builder, and hydration tracker.

**AI Coach**
A conversational coach with full context of your training data. Ask questions, tweak your plan, or get recovery advice — in plain language.

**Gym Finder**
Find fitness centers near you on an interactive map, powered by real-time location data.

**Offline Support**
Log workouts without a connection. Everything syncs automatically when you're back online.
